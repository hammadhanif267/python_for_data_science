Welcome to the Rice Disease Detector

This application helps you identify diseases affecting your rice crops. By simply uploading an image of a diseased leaf, our AI-powered system will analyze the image and provide a diagnosis. Early detection of diseases is crucial for preventing widespread damage and ensuring healthy yields. This tool is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to farmers and agricultural experts alike. We hope this helps in your agricultural endeavors.
